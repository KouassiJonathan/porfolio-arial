Faqs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? I’ve got answers. Whether you're curious about my process, availability, or how we can work together — here are a few things clients often ask.

What services do you offer?

?

I specialize in UI/UX design, branding, and visual design for web and mobile platforms. From wireframes and user flows to polished interfaces and prototypes — I cover the full design journey.

Do you work with startups or only large companies?

?

Both! I enjoy working with early-stage startups just as much as established brands. Each project is a new opportunity to solve problems creatively, no matter the size.

What tools do you use for your design work?

?

Figma is my go-to for UI/UX design, along with tools like Adobe XD, Illustrator, and Photoshop. I also use Notion, Trello, or Slack for project collaboration depending on the client’s workflow.

What is your typical design process?

?

I start by understanding the user's needs and the project goals. From there, I move into wireframing, prototyping, visual design,* and finally handoff with all necessary assets.
